About Our School

Legacy of Quality Education

Renaissance Public School, Gangolihat has been a beacon of quality education in the Kumaon region for over two decades. We blend modern pedagogy with strong values to nurture well-rounded individuals who are ready for the global stage.

Our mission is to provide every child an environment where curiosity is encouraged, potential is unlocked, and character is built — preparing them not just for exams, but for life.
